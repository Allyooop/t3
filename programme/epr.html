What is an Electronic Patient Record (EPR)?

Electronic patient records are...

We are modernising the way we deliver patient care with new technology to record, view and store patient information electronically at all hospital locations, at any time

Lorenzo will initially replace our current Patient Administration System (Patient Centre). We will then be enhancing this system by adding more medical and nursing content, capturing clinical data.

At launch in September 2015, Lorenzo will include:

  • Admission and discharge of patients in Emergency Department
  • Advanced Bed Management
  • Electronic prescribing (initially in one or two pilot sites)
  • Nurse assessment documentation

From September 2015 we will have trained 10,000 staff to use Lorenzo as we go live.

Key Benefits

Statement introducing the key benefits....

Patients deserve and expect the best care. If we are to remain one of the leading NHS hospital trusts in the country, we need to have up-to-date and accurate information about our patients. Currently patient information is collected in a number of different ways and using different computer systems. Our systems are limited in what they can do and they don't always work throughout the hospital and community sites. We often have a very disjointed way of collecting and viewing our patient information. The new system will give us a full patient record, available in multiple locations which will improve how we care for our patients.
