See the new Lorenzo Electronic Patient Record (EPR) in action

Lorenzo Electronic Patient Record (EPR) training dates for medical staff are now available from 15th June â€“ 10th July

The course aim is to enable staff to utilise Lorenzo functionality to see an overview of patient activity and update clinical information. The following Medical Staff should be trained:

  • All medical staff who currently use patient centre
  • All medical staff who want to understand the whiteboard functionality
  • All medical staff who may see patients in A&E
  • Dermatologists and Immunologists who may document allergies in Lorenzo
  • Rotational Junior Doctors who will remain working in STH post September

Staff requiring training should book onto the Medical - Core Medical course on PALMS

Important: No A&E staff should attend, they will have their own courses.

To book a session, please use PALMS. To do so, you must login with your PALMS details

Champions leading the way

More than 400 staff have signed up as Champions to play key roles within the programme

The Transformation Through Technology Champions will receive in depth training to support their areas and help embed our new ways of working. They will also act as ambassadors within their specialist areas and link with the programme team to help resolve issues and escalate concerns.

If you would like to find out more about becoming a Transformation ThroughTechnology Champion, please contact your Operational Change Manager (OCM)
