See the Electronic Document Management System (EDMS) in action

The Electronic Document Management System is being piloted soon and once successfully piloted will be rolled out over a twelve month period

The sessions are aimed at anyone who handles patient casenotes and who will be affected by the introduction of digital case note management. Everyone is welcome to attend
