Transformation Through Technology is a multi-million pound transformational change programme centred on further improving patient care delivered in our hospitals and in the community

Electronic Document Management System (EDMS)

Converting patient casenotes into an electronic format so that they can be viewed and managed on screen 24/7

Find out more

Electronic Patient Record (EPR)

Recording of all patient information electronically on one system. Enabling information sharing, electronic prescribing and electronic clinic management

Find out more

Clinical Portal (CP)

Linking up of all existing computer systems, meaning you only have to log in once to view all patient information systems on one screen.

Find out more

The five year Transformation through Technology programme will see changes in everything we do and bring huge benefits for our patients by bringing faster, more efficient and joined up systems for staff across the Trust to use.

Dr David Throssell, Medical Director, Sheffield Teaching Hospitals NHS Foundation Trust
