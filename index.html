Electronic Document Management System (EDMS)

Converting patient casenotes into an electronic format so that they can be viewed and managed on screen 24/7

Find out more

Electronic Patient Record (EPR)

Recording of all patient information electronically on one system. Will enable information sharing, electronic prescribing and electronic clinic management

Find out more

Clinical Portal (CP)

Linking up of all existing computer systems, meaning you only have to log in once to view all patient information systems on one screen. Enables secure sharing of patient information across the NHS.

Find out more
